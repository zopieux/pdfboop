# v1 - 2026-05-01

Initial release of **pdfboop**, a fully in-browser, offline-first PDF manipulation tool.

- **Page Previews**: Multi-select support, drag-and-drop reordering, Alt+Arrow keys reordering.
- **Page Operations**: Rotate 90° Clockwise/Anticlockwise, Flip Horizontal/Vertical, Delete, Resize/Crop.
- **Files & Assets list**: Inspect and manipulate original PDF files and embedded images.
- **Undo/Redo**: Full history tracking up to 100 operations.
